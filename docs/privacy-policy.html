Privacy Policy

App: Napha: NEPSE Portfolio Tracker  |  Last updated: 29 May 2026

Napha is a personal portfolio tracking app for Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE) investors. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

1. Data We Collect

Napha collects only the data necessary to display your portfolio. All data is stored locally on your device — nothing is sent to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

  • Portfolio and holdings data — your share quantities, average cost (WACC), and profit/loss figures retrieved from MeroShare are stored locally on your device.
  • Live price data — current share prices fetched from the Nepal Stock Exchange (nepalstock.com) are cached locally on your device.
  • Exported files — if you use the export feature, analysis files (JSON/CSV) are saved to your device's private storage.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to display your portfolio within the app. Specifically:

  • Portfolio data retrieved from MeroShare is displayed within the app and stored locally on your device.
  • NEPSE price data is fetched directly from nepalstock.com and displayed in the app.
  • No data is used for advertising, analytics, or profiling.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third party. The only external services your device communicates with are:

  • MeroShare / CDSC (cdsc.com.np, meroshare.cdsc.com.np) — to sync your holdings. This is the same service you log into directly in your browser.
  • Nepal Stock Exchange (nepalstock.com) — to fetch live share prices.

These are the services you are already using as a NEPSE investor. Napha simply automates the process of reading your data from them. We have no affiliation with CDSC or NEPSE.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure local storage.
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on any Napha-operated server.
  • Android backup is disabled for this app — your data is not backed up to Google Drive or any cloud service.
  • MeroShare is accessed through a secure in-app browser (WebView) where you log in directly on CDSC's own website. The app never sees, stores, or transmits your MeroShare credentials.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained on your device until you clear the app's data or uninstall the app. There is no automatic expiry.

6. How to Delete Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time:

  • All data (portfolio cache, settings): Go to Android Settings → Apps → Napha → Storage → Clear Data.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Napha is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
